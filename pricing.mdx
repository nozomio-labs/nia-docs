## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans anytime?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I exceed my limits on the Free plan?">
    Once you reach your monthly limits, you'll need to upgrade or purchase credit packs to continue using those features. Your existing indexed resources will remain accessible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Free plan users use Deep Research, Oracle, and Tracer?">
    Yes! Free plan users can access Deep Research, Oracle, and Tracer by purchasing credit packs. These features are not included in the Free plan's monthly quota, but credits work across all plans.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Deep Research?">
    Deep Research allows Nia to perform comprehensive multi-step research across your indexed sources and the web to answer complex questions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Oracle?">
    Oracle is Nia's advanced reasoning mode for tackling the most challenging questions that require deeper analysis and synthesis.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Tracer?">
    Tracer is Nia's autonomous agent for searching GitHub code without indexing. It delegates tasks to specialized sub-agents for fast, thorough results. Choose between Fast mode (Haiku, quick lookups) and Deep mode (Opus, thorough investigations). Costs 15 credits per job.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in package search?">
    Package search provides access to 150M+ pre-indexed documents from PyPI, NPM, Crates.io, and Go modules. No indexing required!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Team pricing work?">
    Team plans are priced per seat at $50/month. You'll receive a single invoice for your entire team, and you can easily add or remove team members as needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in the Business plan?">
    The Business plan is $99/seat/month and includes higher limits across all features, dedicated support, and is designed for organizations that need more capacity than the Team plan but don't require custom Enterprise infrastructure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does Enterprise include?">
    Enterprise plans include unlimited everything, plus SOC 2 compliance, SLA guarantees, dedicated onboarding and support, and custom infrastructure and security options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?">
    Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions and non-profit organizations. Please reach out to us at arlan@nozomio.com to discuss your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans anytime?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I exceed my limits on the Hacker plan?">
    Once you reach your limit of 3 indexing jobs or 100 queries, you'll need to upgrade to the Cracked plan to continue. Your existing indexed resources will remain accessible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for the Cracked plan?">
    Yes! Solo users get a 7-day free trial to explore all Cracked plan features before committing. No credit card required to start.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in 'unlimited' package search?">
    The Cracked and Startups plans include unlimited searches across 150M+ pre-indexed documents from PyPI, NPM, Crates.io, and Go modules. No indexing required!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does team billing work for Startups plans?">
    Startups plans are priced per developer with managed team billing. You'll receive a single invoice for your entire team, and you can easily add or remove team members as needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'Custom/developer' pricing mean?">
    For growing teams, we offer flexible pricing based on your team size and usage needs. Contact us to discuss a custom plan that fits your requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts?">
    Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions and non-profit organizations. Please reach out to us at arlan@nozomio.com to discuss your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

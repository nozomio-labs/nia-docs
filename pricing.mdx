## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?">
    Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I downgrade?">
    Your indexed repositories remain accessible, but features like private repo access or unlimited API usage will be restricted based on your new plan limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid plans?">
    The Hacker plan serves as an extended free trial with 5 lifetime API requests. This gives you plenty of opportunity to test all features before upgrading.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does billing work for the Startups plan?">
    Startups plan pricing is custom based on your specific needs, team size, and usage requirements. Contact our founder to discuss your requirements and get a personalized quote.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Nia with my existing development tools?">
    Yes! Nia integrates with popular tools like Cursor, Claude Desktop, VS Code (via Cline), Continue.dev, and any application that supports the Model Context Protocol (MCP).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
